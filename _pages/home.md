## Welcome to Vision & AI Lab @ Korea University!

The **Vision & AI (VAI) Lab** at Korea University is dedicated to advancing next-generation AI technologies that integrate visual and linguistic understanding. Our research centers on computer vision and natural language processing, with a strong emphasis on multimodal learning and foundation models. We study the design and training of large-scale models, including **large language models (LLMs)** and **vision-language models (VLMs)**, and develop intelligent systems that address challenging real-world problems. Our research is further expanding into **autonomous driving, Vision-Language-Action (VLA), and robotics**, with the ultimate goal of building embodied AI systems that can actively perceive, understand, and interact with the real world. In collaboration with leading universities, research institutes, and industry partners in Korea and worldwide, we strive to achieve impactful research outcomes through joint projects and academia-industry partnerships.

We are currently looking for undergraduate interns, master’s, and Ph.D. students. We welcome **self-motivated students** who enjoy identifying and solving problems independently. If you are interested, please contact Jungbeom (jbeomlee@korea.ac.kr) with your CV and transcript. We look forward to collaborating with you in our research journey!
